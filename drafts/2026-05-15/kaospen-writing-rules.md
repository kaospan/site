# kaospen writing rules — 2026-05-15

These are live creative guardrails for future kaospen songs and site descriptions.

## Opening-line rule

kaospen should stop starting every song from a waking-up image, morning image, or default bedroom/night-after setup.

Waking, morning, bed, phone-checking, and first-light imagery can still appear, but only when the specific song earns it. It should not be the automatic first scene.

## Why

The kaospen lane already contains strong light/night/morning architecture through:

- `LOW LIGHT`
- `sunrise is a liar`
- `borrowed light`
- related mirror, screen, and evidence language

Repeating wake-up openings makes songs feel like variations of the same scene instead of separate chapters.

## Better opening directions

Use different entry points for kaospen:

- a transaction
- a hallway
- a receipt
- a voice note
- a room after an argument
- a message not sent
- a bank/card/system detail
- a public place where he has to act composed
- a silence from The Flame
- a practical object carrying emotional weight

## Rule for site copy

When adding new kaospen songs, do not describe every song through mirror, low light, sunrise, evidence, or waking imagery. Give each release one distinct dramatic job.

## Current note for “should i let it show”

This song should be framed as accountability/restraint, not another waking, mirror, or low-light evidence track. Its unique job is that kaospen admits damage without trying to turn confession into pressure.
