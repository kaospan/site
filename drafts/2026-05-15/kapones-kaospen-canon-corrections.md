## Tab rule

Avoid duplicating the same explanation across tabs. Each field has a separate job:

- `job`: one sentence explaining why the release exists on the page.
- `audienceRead`: what a normal listener can understand without lore.
- `hiddenRead`: what deeper canon readers should notice.
- `summary`: the shortest public description.
- `meaning`: song meaning only.
- `albumRole`: what it does inside its album lane.
- `projectRole`: what it changes in the full SPEAK ON universe.
- `files`: source/master/status facts only.
- `notes`: metadata, casing, language, continuity warnings only.
